Leadership & Studio

Meet the studio

Architects, designers, engineers and planners working side by side — each project shaped by the whole team.
EV

Elena Vance

Founder & Principal Architect

Elena founded Aurora in 2008 after a decade with leading international studios. She leads design vision across every commission, guided by a lifelong belief in light and proportion.

JR

Julian Reyes

Senior Architect

Julian translates concept into buildable reality. With fifteen years across residential and hospitality, he leads our most complex projects from schematic to site.

ML

Mara Lindqvist

Interior Designer

Mara curates the tactile world of every interior — materials, joinery and light. Her palettes are restrained, warm and unmistakably Aurora.

SC

Project Manager

Sophia keeps every project on course — budgets, timelines and trades aligned. Clients describe working with her as remarkably calm.

Thomas Bergström

Landscape Architect

Thomas dissolves the line between building and garden. His planting and hardscape schemes root every Aurora project firmly in its place.
