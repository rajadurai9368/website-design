Our Story

From a single drawing board to a global practice.

Aurora began in a small studio in 2008, with a conviction that architecture should serve people first. What started as a handful of residential commissions has grown into a practice spanning three continents and every scale of the built environment.

Yet our method hasn't changed. We still begin every project by hand, in conversation and on paper — understanding a site before we shape it, and a client before we design for them.

Today, a close-knit team of architects, interior designers, engineers and planners carries that ethos into homes, workplaces, hotels and civic spaces around the world.

Vision

To design spaces that outlive their moment.

We envision a built world defined by restraint and permanence — buildings that gain meaning with age rather than losing relevance.

Mission

Craft that serves the people within it.

Our mission is to translate each client's aspirations into architecture of clarity and warmth — delivered with rigour, transparency and care at every stage.

Our Values

What guides the work

Clarity

Simple, legible ideas executed with precision. If a decision cannot be explained, it does not belong.

Craft

We obsess over joints, thresholds and details — the small moments that make a space feel resolved.

Integrity

Honest budgets, honest timelines, honest materials. Trust is the foundation of every lasting building.

Context

Every site has a story. We design in dialogue with landscape, climate and culture — never in isolation.

Sustainability

Low-impact materials, passive strategies and buildings designed to last are our default, not an option.

Collaboration

The best work emerges between disciplines. We design with clients, engineers and makers as one team.

Our Journey

A timeline of milestones

2008

The studio opens

Aurora is founded in New York with three architects and a first residential commission on Long Island.

2013

First landmark award

Our Solstice House earns international recognition, establishing the studio's voice in modern residential design.

2017

Expanding disciplines

We open dedicated interior design and urban planning practices, becoming a fully integrated studio.

2021
